Key Pop-out Issue

Applies to Models G10 and Below only

This process is for Models G10 and Below only. Ensure the Subject line contains "Key Pop Out" before proceeding.
⌨️

Step-by-step Decision Flow

Watch alert check → Tier check → Action

  • 1
    Confirm subject line is added with "Key Pop Out".
  • 2
    Check if a watch alert is triggered after submitting the subject line.
  • 3
    No watch alert received? This means the customer is categorised as Tier 1 or Tier 2. Verify in:
    Customer Account → Organisation Account Details → Account Tier
  • 4
    Customer does NOT appear as exempted → Proceed to send a quote.
  • 5
    Customer DOES appear as exempted → Proceed with normal warranty repair.
Source: EMEA Commercial PPS DOA and PDR Process → Key Pop-out Issue

Blank WO Creation – Process Adherence

Critical compliance requirement — do not create blank WOs outside approved scenarios

DO NOT CREATE BLANK WO TO SEND ENGINEER TO TROUBLESHOOT.
We do not have Non DBD Carepacks anymore.
✅

Approved Scenarios for Blank WO

Only these OTC codes are permitted

OTC – 02GService advisory mandating engineer to site without parts (e.g. G11 units with no power)
OTC – 06JCRT approves engineer to go to site to troubleshoot
OTC – 06NADM / SDM / CSM / FSM gives approval to send engineer to troubleshoot
All other scenarios: send Trade Quote to the customer first. Upon acceptance, send Blank WO on 01-Trade.
📋

Standard Flow (Non-approved scenarios)

When customer is unwilling to troubleshoot

  • 1
    Customer is unwilling to troubleshoot.
  • 2
    Send a Trade Quote to the customer.
  • 3
    Upon customer acceptance of the quote, send a Blank WO on 01-Trade.

Secure Boot Certificates

HP vs Enterprise licensing — different handling paths

🛡️

Decision Flow

Device support check → licensing check → routing

  • 1
    Refer first to the HP Business PCs – Prepare for new Windows Secure Boot certificates public document.
  • 2
    Device listed as supported? Follow the steps in the HP support document.
  • 3
    Device NOT listed as supported: Check the licensing type.
  • 4
    Licensing from HP: Follow the WISE Support Document ↗
  • 5
    Licensing from Enterprise: Direct customer to Microsoft Support.
🏢 HP LicensedFollow WISE document process for unsupported devices.
🌐 Enterprise LicensedRedirect customer to Microsoft Support — no action from HP side.

Security Hold – 90 Days Rule

Triggered by duplicate part usage on the same unit

🔒

Security Hold Rules

90-day part repeat restriction

Trigger conditionSame part used within 90 days for the same unit AND a new MO is created for the same part
EffectA security hold is placed on the order
Who can releaseTeam Leader (TL) only
Security holds can only be released by the Team Leader (TL). Do not attempt to bypass or self-release.
